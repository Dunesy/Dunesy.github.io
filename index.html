Who Am I?

I am a graduate from Carleton University with a degree in computer science. I enjoy programming and will code for almost anything that fancies me. Currently I have been working on many of the problems from the Project Euler site, solving each one in sequence. My interests in the field of computer science have always been geared towards problem solving and the various algorithms that exist to solve certain types of problems. These can vary from graph traversal, divide and conquer approaches, genetic algorithms, neural networks, decision tree mapping and so on.
